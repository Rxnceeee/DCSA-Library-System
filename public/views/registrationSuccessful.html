Terms and Conditions
  • Borrowers are fully responsible for the care and timely return of any borrowed book.
  • Borrowers must claim reserved books within 24 hours after booking.
  • Unclaimed books will be released to other students.
  • Borrowers must inspect books before borrowing and report damage to library staff.
  • Failure to settle penalties may result in temporary suspension of library privileges.
  • Disputes will be handled by the librarian or Library Committee; their decision is final.
  • The library reserves the right to revise this policy at any time.
Penalties and Guidelines

1. Damaged Books

  • 1.1 Severe Damage – Replace or pay full value + ₱100 processing fee.
  • 1.2 Mild Damage – Student must repair or pay a repair fee + ₱100.
  • 1.3 Minimal Damage – Attend Library Service (no fee).

2. Lost Books

  • Replace the lost book or pay full value + ₱100 processing fee.

3. Overdue Books

  • Must attend Library Service.
Library Service

This is the penalty for minor violations. Tasks are the following:

  • Organizing books
  • Cleaning shelves
  • Helping maintain the library
