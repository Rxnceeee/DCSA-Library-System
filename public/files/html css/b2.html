Publisher Description

Handbook of Computer Fundamentals by Dr. Nasil Singh Gill provides a comprehensive introduction to the essential concepts of computing. Covering topics such as hardware, software, operating systems, and networking, this book serves as a foundational guide for students and professionals looking to build a strong understanding of computer fundamentals. With clear explanations and practical insights, it is an essential resource for anyone seeking to navigate the ever-evolving world of technology.
