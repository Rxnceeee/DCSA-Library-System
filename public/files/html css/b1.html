21st-Century Literature of the Philippines and the World

By Jesus Z. Menoy

Publisher Description

21st-Century Literature of the Philippines and the World by Jesus Z. Menoy explores contemporary literary works from the Philippines and beyond, highlighting diverse themes, cultures, and perspectives. This book provides an in-depth analysis of modern literary forms, genres, and trends, helping students develop critical thinking and appreciation for global and local narratives. With engaging discussions and activities, it serves as an essential resource for understanding the evolving landscape of literature in the 21st century.
